Who Am I

I am Annie Vo, and I am currently a third-year student majoring in Interactive Arts and Technology at Simon Fraser University. My journey into design began with a love for illustration and storytelling, which naturally led me to explore animation and visual game design. Along the way, I have acquired expertise in user research, interface/visual design, filmmaking, game creation and illustration. My goal is to create immersive visual and narratives that connect with diverse audiences as well as address their pain points during their experience to the products while using creativity to bridge stories, emotions, and interactive experiences.
